Winter resort activities and summer vacation packages

The seasonal diversity of Quebec's climate transforms Esterel Resort into two distinct experiences throughout the year. Winter resort activities include cross-country skiing, snowshoeing, and ice fishing, taking advantage of the region's reliable snowfall and pristine winter conditions. The resort's winter programming emphasizes both active pursuits and cozy indoor experiences, ensuring guests can fully embrace the Canadian winter while maintaining comfort and luxury.

Summer vacation packages focus on water sports activities such as kayaking, paddleboarding, and swimming in the pristine lake waters. The warmer months also feature hiking trails, outdoor dining experiences, and extended spa services on outdoor terraces. According to Travel Canada's official guidance, Quebec's summer season offers optimal conditions for outdoor recreation, with comfortable temperatures and extended daylight hours that maximize vacation experiences.
